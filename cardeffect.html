If you ever think if you loose

Reminder for you today

श्रीभगवानुवाच कर्मण्येवाधिकारस्ते मा फलेषु कदाचन। मा कर्मफलहेतुर्भूर्मा ते सङ्गोऽस्त्वकर्मणि॥ (Bhagavad Gita 2.47) Translation: "You have the right to perform your duty only, but never to its fruits. Do not be motivated by the results of actions, nor be attached to inaction." This verse emphasizes selfless action (Karma Yoga), teaching us to focus on our duties without attachment to success or failure.
